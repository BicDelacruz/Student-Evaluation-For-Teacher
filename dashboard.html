Student Evauluation Guidelines
Please read these guidelines carefully before proceeding with the evaluation process.
1
Be Honest and Objective
Provide truthful feedback based on your actual experience in class. Your responses help improve teaching quality.
2
Be Respectful
Maintain a professional and respectful tone in all comments. Avoid using offensive or inappropriate language.
3
Evaluate All Assigned Teachers
You must complete evaluations for all teachers assigned to your enrolled subjects. Partial submissions are not allowed.
4
One Submission Per Teacher
Once you submit an evaluation for a teacher, it cannot be modified. Review your answers carefully before confirming.
5
Consider the Entire Semester
Base your evaluation on the teacher's performance throughout the entire semester, not just recent experiences.
6
Confidentiality
Your individual responses are confidential. Teachers only receive aggregated results without identifying information.
Important Reminders
• Each teacher must be evaluated completely before submission
• Only one final submission is allowed per teacher
• Your evaluation helps improve the quality of education
• Incomplete evaluation cycles will not be accepted
